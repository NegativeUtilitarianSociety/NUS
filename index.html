Our Mission

Education

Raising awareness about the scale of suffering in nature and human systems through research and outreach.

Vegan Advocacy

Promoting plant-based lifestyles to reduce demand for animal agriculture and its inherent suffering.

Antinatalism

Encouraging ethical considerations about procreation to prevent future suffering of potential sentient beings.
